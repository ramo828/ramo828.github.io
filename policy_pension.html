1. Overview

Təxmini Pensiya Kalkulyatoru is a mobile app designed to provide estimated pension calculations based on user inputs like salary history, contribution rates, and retirement age. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it’s used, who it’s shared with, and your control over your information.

Note: The app does not require account registration, passwords, or full names unless voluntarily provided in free-text fields (not typically required).

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality, analytics, and ad delivery. The categories are:

2.1. Data You Provide Directly

  • Pension Calculation Inputs: Numeric values such as salary, contribution percentages, and retirement age. These are used solely for calculations and not to identify you.
  • Support & Feedback: If you send us an email or feedback, we retain the content and your email address to respond.

2.2. Automatically Collected Data (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data, including:

  • App metrics: sessions, screen views, feature usage, crash reports, and performance metrics.
  • Device metadata: device model, OS version, app version, language, and locale.
  • Anonymous identifiers: issued by Firebase or Google services (not personally identifiable).

Purpose: Improve app stability, diagnose crashes, and enhance user experience. See Firebase Privacy Policy for details.

2.3. Advertising Data (Google Mobile Ads)

We use Google Mobile Ads/AdMob to display ads, which may collect:

  • Advertising ID: A unique identifier for ad personalization (Android).
  • Ad Interactions: Impressions, clicks, and related data to measure ad performance.

You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings or via Google Ad Settings.

3. How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

  • Calculate and display estimated pension amounts based on your inputs.
  • Analyze app usage and performance via Firebase Analytics to fix crashes and improve features.
  • Deliver and measure ads via Google Mobile Ads to keep the app free.
  • Respond to your support requests or feedback via email.

Legal Bases (EEA/UK Users)

For EU/EEA/UK users, our legal bases for data processing are:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide app functionality.
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving the app and analyzing usage, balanced with your rights.
  • Consent: For personalized ads, where required, via in-app or device-level consent (e.g., iOS App Tracking Transparency).

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. Data may be shared with:

  • Google/Firebase: For analytics and crash reporting, acting as our data processor.
  • Google Mobile Ads/Ad Networks: For ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.

Third parties have their own privacy policies. Review them at Firebase and Google Ads.

Analytics and ad data are retained per third-party provider policies (e.g., Firebase and Google Ads). Support emails or feedback are kept only as needed to respond, unless you request deletion.

To request deletion of data we hold directly, contact ramosoft94@gmail.com.

6. Your Choices & Opting Out

Analytics

To stop Firebase Analytics data collection:

  • Uninstall the app to halt data collection on your device.
  • Use device-level privacy controls or Google account settings to limit analytics.

Ads & Advertising ID

Control ad personalization:

  • Android: Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads to reset or delete your Advertising ID.
  • Manage ad preferences at Google Ad Settings.
  • iOS: Use App Tracking Transparency to control tracking across apps.
